Hibachi Style Fried Rice

This hibachi-style fried rice recipe is quick and easy and sure to be a big hit.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup chopped grilled chicken (Optional)
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat a wok or nonstick skillet over medium-high heat; add olive oil.
  3. Cook and stir eggs in the hot oil until cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Add chicken, green onion, and garlic to scrambled eggs; cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
  5. Mix rice into egg mixture; cook and stir until rice is warmed, about 2 minutes.
  6. Add soy sauce; cook and stir until rice mixture is evenly coated, about 3 minutes.
