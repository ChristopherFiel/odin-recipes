A Freshly Made Filipino Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Directions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.
  3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown, about 3 to 5 minutes
  4. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.
  5. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
